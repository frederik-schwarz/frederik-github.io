Learning Plan Bootcamp

What are my biggest strengths and limitations for Bootcamp?

I think my biggest strength for Bootcamp will be resilience and people skills. I think my biggest weakness will be my ability to stay engaged during tasks I don’t find as enjoyable.

My biggest non-technical challenge?

Preparing myself / portfolio for a job interview. (I don’t like writing)

What team skills would I like to see developed?

I would like to see myself becoming an active voiced member in a problem-solving environment. And further down the line even a leader.

What are my expectations of a Bootcamp team?

My expectations of the Bootcamp team are to be open to hearing questions/ideas and help solve these without judgment.

What are the expectations for myself at Bootcamp?

I expect myself to try my very best and not shy away from any tasks. I don’t tend to shy away but in a new stressful environment I expect myself to keep this attitude. I also expect myself to stick at problems and not throw in the towel after a few attempts. Challenge myself and be open to the idea of getting stuck. On a social level, I expect myself to treat everyone with respect and be open to helping others if I can.
